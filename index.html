About Me

Hello and welcome to my first, complete website featuring myself and my work! I am 30 years old and a new father on a grand adventure to build a better future for my family and myself. I have a vast background in different types of education and learning, however, none will prove to be as lucrative and rewarding as the University of Arizona Coding Bootcamp.

I was born and raised on a little island off the coast of Portugal, called Sao Miguel. I was fortunate to experience many European adventures including North Africa and Israel due to the proximity of my childhood home. I have experienced many different cultures, adventures and, my favorite of all, many amazing culinary dishes. A future in coding and programming will allow me to return to Europe permantely while working remotely. It will offer me the opportunity to create a life for my family similar to that I was fortunate to experience growing up. And this program is the doorway to such dreams and aspirations!
